Webinar: Follow Through: Why People Don't and How You Can

During this interactive webinar Mark Goulston, M.D., makes the case for the importance of follow through and provides the steps necessary to follow through on your commitments to gain respect and trust from others. In this webinar you will learn why people don't follow through, why people make promises they might not keep, the importance of integrity, and the steps necessary for consistent follow through.

Webinar

Signature Event: Advice & Strategies to Achieve Your Business Dreams

Join us to learn strategies and resources to help your business grow. Keynote speaker Chris Rabb, author of Invisible Capital: How Unseen Forces Shape Entrepreneurial Opportunity, will discuss the power of “invisible capital” which represents those unseen forces that dramatically impact business viability when hard work, a great idea and a good attitude simply aren't enough. Rabb puts forth concrete and effective ways business owners and their advocates can build and grow sustainable enterprises amid these unseen forces created by society's uneven playing field.

Following the keynote, hear from a panel of dynamic speakers about their business growth journeys as well as tips to ensure your business is prepared to expand.

Speakers include:

  • Hernan Guaracao, CEO and Publisher, AL DÍA NewsMedia
  • Kareem Richardson, Solutions Architect, Verizon Wireless
  • Steven Singer, President, Steven Singer Jewelers
  • Solomon Wheeler, Lender Relations and Economic Development Specialist, US Small Business Administration
