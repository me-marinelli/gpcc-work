Membership 101

Membership 101 provides a quick but comprehensive look at the Chamber's benefits, services and programs. It provides an opportunity to network with members and meet the Member Relations team — a dedicated group of Chamber staff assigned to help your company get the most out of your Chamber membership. Come prepared with a 20-second commercial describing what you do and the benefits of your product or service and bring promotional materials to display on the share table.

Young Professionals Network Event

YPN Networking at NextFab

Join members of YPN at NextFab to learn about “Philadelphia's gym for innovators” and hear from Evan Malone, President of NextFab.

NextFab is a DIY prototyping facility with an innovative membership model. Its mission is to foster innovation and manufacturing through access to next-generation digital design and fabrication technologies and services. Bring plenty of business cards and be prepared to expand your network, as well as make new contacts with fellow young professionals in the region.

Sponsorship Opportunities

10/15 — Annual Meeting

Sponsorship opportunities are available for our signature Annual Meeting event, where 1,400 business professionals, leaders and elected officials gather to network and learn about the Chamber's accomplishments over the past year and preview agenda items for the coming year. This year we are welcoming Denis O'Brien, Senior Executive Vice President, Exelon Corporation; CEO, Exelon Utilities as our incoming Board Chairman.

11/5 — Excellence Awards Honoring Small Businesses

Sponsorship opportunities are available for the 32nd Annual Excellence awards where we celebrate the accomplishments and contributions small businesses make to our regional economy. The Awards are presented in 11 categories, and honorees are nominated by their peers and selected by a panel of distinguished business owners and advisors.

For more information about these or other event sponsorship opportunities, contact Tara Orio at torio@gpcc.com or 215-790-3819.
